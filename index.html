I went through my dad's old stuff today, looking through his hunting gear. It always made me uncomfortable to have all these guns in my house, but now I've never been more thankful. I took the loaded shotgun and opened the skylight in the attic and climbed onto the roof. I looked around to see if there were any cars nearby. I prayed that no one actually needed to use it and took my aim on a bright red one about 5 houses over. I hoped that the loud noise from the gun would only momentarily catch the zombies' attention before going towards the car. Finally, I took a deep breath and pulled the trigger. The noise was deafening! The car started ringing, and I watched as the zombies started slowly making their way towards it. It worked! Now I just needed to make my escape.
